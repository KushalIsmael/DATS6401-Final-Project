Paycheck Protection Program

The Paycheck Protection Program is part of theis a loan designed to provide a direct incentive for small businesses to keep their workers on payroll. It was created as part of the Coronavirus Aid, Relief, and Economic Security Acts (CARES Act) on March 27, 2020. Read more about PPP on the US Treasury's website at https://home.treasury.gov/policy-issues/cares/assistance-for-small-businesses
